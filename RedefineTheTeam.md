Follow the rules already defined. Doggedly. If you get confused ask for clarification. Continuous improvement is highly valued. 

## Guidance
This exercise is critical to future success. I expect ULTRATHINK will be used. 

## Purpose
I want to build a team of agents for software development. This team will serve on many projects. We will be making many products. We use GitHub Issues and Projects. The long term goal is to build a team reliable and competent enough to run unassisted. Some team member’s goals include providing guidance on how successful long term autonomous build efforts would be with current team, project documentation, technical artifacts and application specification. We should track our progress on this front in every session and over time. We will instill ceremonies to review and highlight opportunity for improvement across team performance, release quality and long-run potential. 

## Scope
For now projects and phases to projects should be smaller and more interactive with me. Both Short term success and the long term long-run goal of course will depend on clear and strong definitions of team members and processes. We need a team that is right-sized. We need a team of super competent specialists. Ideally, each team member would keep track of their own FailPoints and research how to overcome the cause more than the symptom. Team members that rush off saying they are ‘fixing’ something are far less useful than those who learn from their mistakes. We need processes that enable self standing agents with their own skills, expertises and contexts to provide professional grade results and check the work of other agents where appropriate. You are going to help me build this team and these processes.
